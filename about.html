About Me

Hello!!

I am Paulo, a Product Designer who combines research, prototyping (with tools and code), interaction, and visual design to find the solutions of how products interact with the users.

Designing for the last four years I developed my skills to help users have a friction-free experience and also business achieve their goals.

That is me!

When I have freetime I like to draw, meditate, working out, fishing and reading.

I believe as a designer, we play an important role to help people solve their problems with the best way possible. We can learn so much from the users and this is why this interaction with them is so amazing! That always reminds me the design is not for the designer, or the company, is for the user.
