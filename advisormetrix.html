Advisor Metrix, Financial Business Intelligence

Advisor Metrix empowers advisors to focus equally on efforts that drive their business and client relationships forward. Creating a destination for essential business data, Advisor Metrix improves accessibility to key client and account details, enabling financial professionals to more accurately monitor and evaluate the performance of their practice and improve efficiency within their teams.

View Website

To be able to help advisors to track their time spent with clients, a new time tracker was added to the Platform, that way was much easier to see how clients and their doubts were taking more time to be answered to improve internal processes.

A call log system was also designed to help the advisors to save important information about the conversations they had with the clients, that way they can quickly access details they discussed with each client and also know where the point the conversation ended in the last call.

Insightful reports were the main goal for this product to help generate metrics visually. So when the advisor is checking the customer account they can easily simulate, consult and share the data in a more friendly way with customers.
