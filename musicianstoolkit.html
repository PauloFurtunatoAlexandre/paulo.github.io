Musician’s Toolkit, e-learning

Musician’s Toolkit is an online music education website designed to supplement the efforts of music educators, music learners, and music makers. Providing video courses, teaching by nationally recognized teachers and industry professionals, will give individuals an edge in their music learning, and provide teachers with resources to help them flip the classroom, and increase post-lesson engagement. Musician’s Toolkit courses cover all aspects of music education including Instrument maintenance and fundamentals, Vocal Fundamentals, Music Theory, Songwriting and Composition, Music Business, Recording, and Composition. Design Artifacts.

View Website

After interviewing users, we found out it would br more interesting to use different segments of e-learning services depending on user needs, for example, guitar (as guitar being the most popular search), vocal music, teachers, strings, band, commercial music, find the fun in music and elementary for learning the basics of many instruments.

Based on that discovery, the plans also were based more on what is the user's goals. Three main groups were created to cover all the main topics above: Individuals (Single User or Families), School (One single license can register the teacher and all his/her students), and Studio (Up to 50 users with unlimited access to all the library in one single license).
