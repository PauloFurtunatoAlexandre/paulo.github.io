RISE Cannabis, eCommerce

RISE Cannabis is the revenue branch of Green Thumb Industries, which means they cultivate and also sell cannabis for recreational and medical usage. Design Artifacts.

My Contributions: Design, Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, Creative-Workshops, Front-end Code

View Website

RISE Cannabis is aiming to become the number one Cannabis Dispensary in the US. To achieve that goal, they are differentiating themselves from other cannabis dispensaries in the market by changing the way people experience shopping it and also adding new elements as shopping by feelings, shopping by activities, fit guide to help newcomers to understand what they are looking for, new product cards' concept to help people see those elements, and also educate them while shopping. New website Information Architecture organization to help the users navigate more instinctively, finding more relevant information about their needs and also informing discoveries about Cannabis. Working together with a designer strategist I redesigned and validated Personas, Profile, Filters, Product Menu Page, Product Details Page, Dispensary Page based on user location/search, and Global Age gate. New Cart Preview, refined the language and tone to better resonate with the community and culture values, also emphasizing the benefits of membership. Added the concept of order history, and order favorites for an optimal experience.

New art direction and prototypes (Figma “static” prototypes and a dynamic prototype with code validated with users on every single sprint) working with another UI designer. Usability tests and research with 5 users at least per round. In the last sprint, we have done a proof of concept and usability test with budtenders, users, and members. Showed metrics and value brought of discoveries to the stakeholders.

Redesigned Desktop and mobile using Material UI.
